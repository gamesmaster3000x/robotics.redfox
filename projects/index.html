First Tech Challenge 2019/20

Our latest project is the First Tech Challenge - an event in which competing robots are tested on a variety of "skills" such as maneuverability, speed, agility etc.

Teams must build their robots from scratch, within a set of guidelines with the aim to complete a given challenge - in the 2019/20 competition this means building the tallest tower from the most valuable bricks in the time limit.
